MotoRental is a motorbike rental service 

This android application allows you to rent motorcycles where ever you 
are fast and clean with no hassle. 

In this application you are able to add customer details, make your rental 
and it also allows you to view all our customers which have or still are renting 
our motorcycles.

On the contact us page you are able to view the sales team avaible and rquest them
personally by name when contacting us.
